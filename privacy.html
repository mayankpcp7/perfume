Transaction information may also be collected when using third-party business partners, whose privacy policies will apply when they collect your personal data directly.

If you receive a call or email claiming to be from AMBAR ESSENCE requesting sensitive data like your card PIN or net/mobile banking password, do not share such information. If you have already revealed it, report it immediately to an appropriate law enforcement agency.

We use personal data to provide services requested by you. For marketing purposes, we will provide you the ability to opt out.
